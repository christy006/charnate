About Us

Who We Are

Charnate is a platform for corporations to donate to nonprofits and community organizations. Users are able to communicate with others interested in their category of work. Charnate believes that an overwhelming amount of nonprofits struggle with gaining funding and sponsors. We hope to aid our community through the mutual benefit of sponsorships.

Data

42% of nonprofits do not have the right mix of financial resources to thrive and be effective in the next three years

20% of nonprofit funding in the United States is unrestricted

81% of nonprofit leaders say access to capital is their biggest challenge

88% of demand for services keeps rising as 57% can't meet it
